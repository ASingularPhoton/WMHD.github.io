DJ Services

WMHD offers DJ services for on-campus events. Fill out our rental request form. WMHD personnel will set up equipment and play requested music.

Equipment Rentals

WMHD has speakers, microphones, mixers, lighting systems, and more available for free to Rose-Hulman students and organizations. Request rentals via our rental form.

Recording Studio

The Recording Studio is free to rent for recording or practice. It includes a keyboard, drum kit, bass amp, microphones, music stands, and cables.

Podcast Studio

The Podcast Studio is free to book for voice-based recording. High-quality microphones are provided. For special needs, consult our Podcasting Director.
