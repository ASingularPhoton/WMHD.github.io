About WMHD Radio

WMHD Radio is a student-run radio that offers online music, equipment rentals, and DJ services for the Rose-Hulman Institute of Technology community. Our studio is located in the basement of BSB Hall, room 032.

Club Meetings

WMHD Online Radio has general club meetings in the basement of BSB, room 032. The time for next quarter is still tentative. In these meetings, we discuss/listen to music, plan events, stream live radio shows, and run workshops on DJing, equipment setup, and recording.

Room Info

We have four main rooms in BSB basement: the Broadcasting Room (BSB032), Recording Studio (BSB030), Podcasting Studio (BSB028), and Music Lounge (BSB022). We also have a storage room for CDs, records, and equipment. Every Rose-Hulman student is welcome to use the rooms. See Services for info about the recording and podcast studios.
